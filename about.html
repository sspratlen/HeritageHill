About Heritage Hill

Our story, our heart, and what we believe.

Our Story

Rooted in Papillion, Reaching the World

Heritage Hill Church has been a part of the Papillion community for over two decades. What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant family of faith — diverse in background, united in purpose.

We're not a perfect church. We're a church of imperfect people who believe in a perfect God — and who are on the journey together, step by step, day by day.

Mission & Vision

Found. Filled. Freed. Forged.

Four words that capture who we are and where we're headed — and four pathways to help you take your next step.

Found by God

Weekend Services

God doesn't wait for us to find our way to Him — He pursues us. Every Sunday we gather around that reality, through worship, Scripture, and the presence of His Spirit. Whether you're just exploring faith or have been walking with Jesus for years, our services are a place where you can encounter the God who already knows your name.

Filled by the Spirit

Small Groups

We weren't designed to do life alone — and the Holy Spirit does some of His best work in the context of real community. Small Groups are where faith goes deeper: honest conversations, shared meals, and people who know you beyond Sunday. Joining a group is one of the most life-changing steps you can take at Heritage Hill.

Freed to Live Like Jesus

Growth Track Coming Soon

Jesus didn't just save us from something — He saved us for something. Salvation is the starting line, not the finish line. Through intentional discipleship, we walk people through the process of breaking old patterns, renewing their minds, and stepping into the freedom that Jesus purchased for them — freedom to actually live the way He lived.

Forged for Mission

Impact Teams Coming Soon

Every follower of Jesus is called to more than a seat on Sunday. We were put here — in this city, in this neighborhood, at this moment — on purpose. Heritage Hill exists to send people out: to serve Papillion, to love our neighbors, and to reach the world. There's a place for you to be part of something bigger than yourself.

What to Expect When You Visit

We know showing up somewhere new can feel intimidating. Here's what you can expect when you come to Heritage Hill for the first time.

1

You'll be welcomed

Our greeters are real people who are genuinely glad you're here — not scripted salespeople. You'll get directions to wherever you need to go.

2

Kids are taken care of

Safe, fun, and age-appropriate kids ministry is available for children from birth through 5th grade at every service.

3

Come as you are

No dress code, no performance required. You'll find people in everything from jeans to dress shirts — what matters is that you're here.

4

No pressure

We won't ask you to stand up, introduce yourself, or put your hand in the air. Come, experience, and decide if Heritage Hill is your home.
