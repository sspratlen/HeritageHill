The Call

You Were Made to Lead.

Scripture is full of men who built something that outlasted them.

Moses

God took a man hiding in the wilderness and forged him into a deliverer. Moses built a nation from slaves into a covenant people — not through his own strength, but by staying close to the One who called him.

Jesus

He didn't build a movement with power or politics. He gathered twelve ordinary men, poured into them for three years, and changed the course of history. The model was intentional: leadership multiplied through relationship.

Paul

Everywhere Paul went, he built teams. Timothy, Silas, Titus — men raised up and sent out. He planted communities in every city that carried the gospel forward long after he moved on. That's the power of team leadership.

Leadership isn't a title. It's a calling.

About the Retreat

A Weekend Built for Men Who Want to Grow

This retreat is a weekend built for men who want to grow — as husbands, fathers, teammates, and leaders. Whether you lead a family, a team at work, or a ministry at church, God has called you to build something that lasts.

Come expecting challenge, brotherhood, and practical tools you can take home and use Monday morning.
